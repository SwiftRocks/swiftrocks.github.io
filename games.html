Bruno's Top 10 Video Games of All Time

Last updated: 13 Aug 2023

For readability reasons, I've decided to mention most franchises only once. When you see an individual title mentioned (e.g "Metal Gear Solid 2"), assume that I'm stating that the other games in that franchise are also good games, with the one being mentioned being the best overall.

  1. Super Mario 64 (N64)
  2. Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons Of Liberty (PS2)
  3. Half-Life 2 (PC)
  4. Team Fortress 2 (PC)
  5. Portal (PC)
  6. Bloodborne (PS4)
  7. Super Mario Odyssey (Switch)
  8. Fallout: New Vegas (PC)
  9. Castlevania: Symphony of The Night (PS1)
  10. Super Smash Bros. Brawl (Wii)

Runner-ups (in no particular order): DOOM Eternal, The Talos Principle, Dark Souls, Elden Ring, Devil May Cry 5, Garry's Mod, Hades, Diddy Kong Racing, Halo 3, Overlord, VVVVVV, The Witcher 3, Mass Effect 2, Street Fighter 4, God of War, StarCraft, WarCraft 3, World of Warcraft, Hollow Knight, Cyberpunk 2077, Skyrim, Metroid Prime 3: Corruption, Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas, Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance, Bastion, Counter-Strike 1.6, Dota 2, League of Legends, Call Of Duty 4: Modern Warfare, Deus Ex: Human Revolution, Ori and the Blind Forest, Left 4 Dead, Killing Floor, Mirror's Edge, Serious Sam, Sonic The Hedgehog 2, Super Meat Boy, Mario Party, Super Mario World, Goldeneye: 007, Pokémon Silver/Gold, StarFox 64, Mario Kart 8, Banjo-Kazooie, Pokémon Stadium, Donkey Kong Country, Mortal Kombat: Armageddon, Pikmin 4, The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, Shadow of the Colossus, The Last of Us
