Bruno's Top 10 Video Games of All Time

Last updated: 19 Apr 2025

For readability reasons, I've decided to mention most franchises only once. When you see an individual title mentioned (e.g "Metal Gear Solid 2"), assume that I'm stating that the other games in that franchise are also good games, with the one being mentioned being the best overall.

  1. Super Mario 64 (N64, 1996)
  2. Half-Life 2 (PC, 2004)
  3. Portal (PC, 2007)
  4. Counter-Strike (PC, 1999)
  5. Astro Bot (PS5, 2024)
  6. Team Fortress 2 (PC, 2007)
  7. Castlevania: Symphony of The Night (PS1, 1997)
  8. Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons Of Liberty (PS2, 2001)
  9. Baldur's Gate 3 (PC, 2023)
  10. Metaphor: ReFantazio (PC, 2024)
